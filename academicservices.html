Academic Services

NTCs

PULS sells NTCs for the following courses:

  • PHGY 210
  • PHGY 311
  • PHGY 312
  • PHGY 313
  • PHGY 314
  • PATH 300
PULS hires student writers to take detailed lecture notes as well as student editors to edit those custom notes. Lecture notes are usually available for print at CopiEUS in McConnell Engineering within 3 days. Check the CopiEUS Website to see the most recent lecture available for print!

Prices:

  • Full semester: $40.00
  • Specific section: variable

Payment methods: Cash or e-Transfer






Course Packs


PULS sells course packs for PHGY209, PHGY210 and PHGY312 (renal section only).

The PHGY209 and PHGY 210 course packs are not mandatory and contain all of the lecture slides from each lecturer, with no material outside the scope of the lectures.

The PHGY312 course pack contains material and practice questions pertaining to the renal unit. It is mandatory for the course, as it contains material that is not presented during lectures but that may appear on exams!

Prices:

  • PHGY 209: $35.00
  • PHGY 210: $35.00
  • PHGY 312: $15.00

Payment methods: Cash or e-Transfer






Rentals


PULS provides free rentals for lab coats, safety glasses and adapters. Stop by the PULS office to pick up what you need: we only require that you leave your McGill ID with us until the item is returned!

We have 1 Thunderbolt/Mini Display Port-to-VGA adapter (Macs) and 1 HDMI-to-VGA adapter (PCs).

Rentals are on a first come, first served basis. Message our Facebook Page to reserve the item you need in advance!

Synapse Program


Every year, PULS pairs each U1 student with a U3 mentor. U1 mentees can reach out to their mentor to discuss course selection, programs within the Physiology department, research, career opportunities after graduation, etc. PULS also organizes 1-2 Synapse Events per year to allow mentors and mentees to get to know each other in-person.

Any U3 students interested in becoming Synapse mentors should contact a U3 representative at the beginning of the academic year!
